Learn the Fundamentals

This course is a comprehensive introduction to the history of world art from 3,000,000 BC to the present.

The larger goal of this course is to provide the learner with visual literacy across cultural divides. The more specific goals of this course are for learners to develop a critical understanding of art historical terms, methodologies, art objects and mediated environment meanings, within specific global, cultural, political, economic and religious contexts.

Discover the stories
